Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI Credits?

AI Credits power the advanced features of ChatBridge, such as scanning, rewriting, comparing models, and executing agent workflows. Each action consumes a small number of credits depending on its complexity (e.g., a simple summary vs. a multi-model side-by-side comparison).

What happens if I run out of credits?

If you exceed your monthly quota, you can either upgrade to a higher tier, buy credit top-ups (available in settings), or bring your own API keys to continue using ChatBridge without credit limits.

Can I use my own API key (BYOK)?

Yes! ChatBridge supports Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) for all major providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and OpenRouter). If you use your own API key, advanced features will not consume your monthly ChatBridge credits.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time with a single click from the settings page. You will retain access to your plan's benefits until the end of your current billing cycle.

Which plan is best for me?

The Free plan is great for light users who need core utility features. The Pro plan is the most popular, tailored for daily power users, developers, and creators. The Max plan is designed for researchers and professionals with heavy AI workflows who require fact-checking, persistent memory, and agentic automations.
